This position requires that you commute to the San Jose, CA office at least 3 days a week.


The successful applicant will be performing work in a FedRAMP environment, and therefore, must be a U.S. Person (i.e., U.S. citizen, U.S. national, lawful permanent resident, asylee, or refugee). This position may also perform work that the U.S. government has specified can only be performed by a U.S. citizen on U.S. soil.


Meet the Team

Cisco’s SD-WAN vAnalytics team builds Cisco vAnalytics — a cloud-based analytics platform that delivers deep insights into application and network performance across the SD-WAN fabric. We’re looking for a seasoned Software Engineer to join our Cloud Infrastructure & Services team and help drive innovation at scale.


Your Impact

In this role, you will design, develop secure cloud infra services and lead FedRamp compliance efforts, serving as the lead to engagements and audits. You’ll review and define FedRAMP/Security needs, and recommend configurations With your expertise in AWS cloud Infra, data processing, cloud security, encryption, and vulnerability management.

  • Secure Architecture Knowledge: Design and implement cloud security Infra services architectures that meet security requirements.
  • Cloud Security Expertise: Recommend secure configurations for cloud services (e.g., AWS GovCloud, Azure Government), balancing FedRAMP and security best practices and DevOps.
  • Building large scale real-time streaming pipelines using AWS or Kafka or Spark
  • As part of this role, you will also be responsible for developing & maintaining services in a FedRAMP compliant environment
  • FedRAMP-Specific Guidance: Advise on requirements like container scanning (e.g., runtime security, image integrity) and vulnerability management (e.g., risk prioritization, scan schedules).


Minimum Qualifications

  • 6+ years of software development experience with an expert-level of experience coding in Python, and strong AWS, Cloud Infra, and database development experience
  • Design and implementation experience with cloud security infrastructure services
  • Security compliance experience with AWS cloud infrastructure development
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment, and Dev Ops
  • Bachelor degree in Computer Science or similar


Preferred Qualifications

  • Ability to articulate design choices based on an expert understanding of the product, technology, and ROI
  • Knowledge and use of Docker, cloud technologies (AWS, Infra) security
  • Building large scale real-time streaming pipelines using AWS or Kafka or Spark
  • Able to scope work accurately and provide reliable estimates for project milestones and completion
  • Strong team collaboration and communication skills

Employees on sales plans earn performance-based incentive pay on top of their base salary, which is split between quota and non-quota components. For quota-based incentive pay, Cisco typically pays as follows:

.75% of incentive target for each 1% of revenue attainment up to 50% of quota;

1.5% of incentive target for each 1% of attainment between 50% and 75%;
